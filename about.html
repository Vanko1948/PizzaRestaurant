Нашият ресторант

Pizza Palace is a family-owned pizza restaurant that has been serving delicious, handmade pizza for over 20 years. We use only the freshest ingredients and make our dough and sauce from scratch daily. Our menu includes a wide variety of classic and specialty pizzas, as well as salads, sandwiches, and desserts.
